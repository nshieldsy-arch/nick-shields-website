# Welcome to My Blog

Thanks for stopping by. I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and I'm excited to finally have a place to share my thoughts.

## What I'll Be Writing About

I plan to cover a range of topics that I'm passionate about:

- **AI & the Future of Software** — The AI landscape is evolving at a staggering pace. I'll be sharing my observations on where the industry is heading, what it means for product builders, and how startups can navigate this shift.

- **Data Products & Go-to-Market** — Having spent years scaling data products from zero to millions in ARR, I've picked up a few lessons along the way. I'll dig into product strategy, GTM execution, and what it takes to build products that customers actually want.

- **B2B Startups & Advisory** — As someone who actively advises early-stage AI and B2B software companies, I'll share frameworks and insights that founders might find useful.

- **Other Interests** — Expect the occasional post about geopolitics, books I'm reading, or whatever else is on my mind.

## Why Now?

I've always believed that writing is one of the best ways to clarify your thinking. After years of consuming great content from others, it felt like the right time to start contributing to the conversation.

If any of this sounds interesting, stay tuned — there's plenty more to come.

And as always, feel free to [reach out](mailto:nshieldsy@gmail.com) if you want to chat.
